About

John Northrup

Compelling Media - Powerful Stories - Positive Results

John Northrup is the founder of Säntis Creative Group (SCG) and he has traveled to more than 40 countries producing media projects since 1985. At SCG, we enjoy working collaboratively to create powerful personal stories.

Our clients include a variety of commercial and non-profit organizations including Lockheed Martin, Dutch National Television, The Jesus Film Project, ESPN, Trans World Radio and Purpose Driven Publishing. Recently, John worked with the Billy Graham Evangelistic Association covering International events, developing media stories for their website and producing segments for their television specials.

Säntis Creative Group is based out of Asheville, North Carolina.
